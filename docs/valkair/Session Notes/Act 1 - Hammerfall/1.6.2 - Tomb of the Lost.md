# 1.6.2 - Tomb of the Lost

*The Speaker sealed the way home behind you, and left you to find an empty tomb with Hannah's name on it.*

The stone ground shut behind you, the Speaker's hollow face the last thing you saw before the barrier slid home. No way back. You stood in the Tomb of the Lost, walls weeping cold, and pressed on because forward was the only direction left.

A door locked itself at your backs as you went, sealing the Thrandurassil tomb for good. Ahead, a great central pyramid held the tombs and statues of Queen Aduni and King Hadran. Atop it, one of the Chosen was waiting with a pet demon, and at his word the dormant Blood Elves stirred to life around you. You fought across the levels of it, through darkness and dispelled darkness, and put them down, though the demon slipped away into the black. Ygritte's captors had already gone deeper and lost you. But two things in that chamber mattered more than the fight. Moving Aduni's statue uncovered a hidden hatch below. And set in the wall stood a vast sealed door carved with the Pilgrimage of the Eight, eight god-symbols missing from its face. Beyond a secret passage you found Hannah's own tomb, and her sarcophagus was empty.

## Remember For Next Time

- **No way back:** the Speaker sealed you into the catacombs. The only road is deeper.
- **The sealed door:** a great door carved with the Pilgrimage of the Eight, missing eight divine symbols. It will not open without them.
- **Hannah's empty tomb:** her sarcophagus is empty. Whatever she is now, she did not stay buried.
- **The demon escaped:** the Chosen's babau slipped away, and the captors are still ahead with Ygritte.
