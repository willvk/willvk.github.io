# Queen Aduni Thrandurassil
*Previous High Queen of the Elves - House Thrandurassil*

Mother of [[Caerdan]] and previous High Queen, ruling from [[Dres Elas]] before her son's election. Her husband was a young [[Netherese]] elf who would go on to become Caerdan's father.

Aduni, her direct retinue, and the rest of the Thrandurassil aristocracy disappeared from the Palatial district shortly before the fall of Dres Elas to the Pact's forces. Her fate is unknown.
