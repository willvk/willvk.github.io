# Orgrim
*Ork war leader - Pact representative*

Orgrim leads the united Ork tribes and speaks for them on [[The Pact]]. He allied with [[Ivar]] to form the Great Heathen Army and helped take [[Hammerfall]] from the elves.

He is one of the strongest faces of Ork power in the city, responsible for guards, city defense, and keeping the Pact from turning on itself.
