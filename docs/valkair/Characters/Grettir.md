# Grettir
*"The Leech" - Loki's Hand surgeon - deceased*

A scarred surgeon and one of Kvarrac's most trusted Fingers in [[Loki's Hand]], known for the bone saws on his belt and a promise: everyone loses someone eventually, and that's what he's for, if you have the coin. Black market in monster parts, and for the right price, any other kind.

When [[Brelka]] made her move against Kvarrac at the Black Tide Tavern, Grettir stood with her — transforming alongside her into something no longer entirely human. He died defending the coup, killed by [[Varka]] in the wreckage of the Tavern.
