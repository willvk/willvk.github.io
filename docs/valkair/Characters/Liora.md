# Liora
*Riven's childhood friend*

One of three friends [[Riven]] grew up with in the orphanage in [[The Middens]] — the one whose warm smile and gentle heart could light up even the darkest day. Gave Riven a necklace, a small treasured keepsake, in the years before the orphanage was exposed as a trap.

When their captors closed in, Liora helped create the diversion that let Riven escape with [[Torran]] and [[Etta]], while she stayed behind. Her fate since is unknown. The necklace she gave him became [[Liora's Whisper]].
