# Etta
*Riven's childhood friend*

One of three friends [[Riven]] grew up with in the orphanage in [[The Middens]] — smart and resourceful, with a deep fascination for [[Ullr]] and the legends of Norse archery and hunting. When their captors closed in, Etta pressed an ordinary hornbow into Riven's hands and told him to let it protect him. The bow would go on to become Winter's Vigil, later sacrificed and reforged into [[Tyr's Measure]]. Her fate since is unknown.
