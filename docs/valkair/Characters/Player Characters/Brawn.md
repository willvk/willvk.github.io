# Brawn
*Orc - Fighter/Monk - Guild backstory*

Brawn grew up in Murgrog, where orcs used grappling techniques to control the large animals they rode. He made a name for himself as a wrestler before the Guild bribed and set him up, sending him to prison for five years.

In the quiet of that cell he found monk ways. After the events in Hammerfall, he sacrificed his hand to [[Tyr]] and now fights with a living chain-link replacement.
