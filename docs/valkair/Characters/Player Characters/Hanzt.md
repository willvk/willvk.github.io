# Hanzt
*Human - Alchemist/Rogue - Guild backstory*

Hanzt was known in the Guild as the Pathogen, an alchemical interrogator and enforcer. He grew up on the street, found family in the Guild, and learned quickly how to make people talk.

That life ended when he was ordered to break an innocent family and chose instead to free them. He is now on the run and trying to prove there is something worth redeeming in him.
