# Hannah
*aka Hannah the Forsaken - central antagonist of Season 1*

Hannah was cast out by the elves and locked away in a magical prison beneath [[Dres Elas]]. The party freed her in session 1.2.0 by breaking the ruby keystone that held her soul in place.

She is tied to [[The Chosen]] and the force behind their rise. What began as exile turned into a blood-soaked cult, fiendish power, and a long reach into the politics of Hammerfall.
