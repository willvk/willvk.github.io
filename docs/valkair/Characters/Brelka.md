# Brelka
*aka "The Riptide" - sailor-turned-smuggler, former Loki's Hand Finger*

Brelka ran sea-based contraband through Mongeet: banned elixirs, hallucinogenic seaweed, and whatever else could be sold without asking too many questions.

She was one of Kvarrac's Fingers in [[Loki's Hand]] and helped hold the Shallows together until the coup at [[The Black Tide Tavern]] broke the Hand apart. The party killed her in session 1.2.8.
