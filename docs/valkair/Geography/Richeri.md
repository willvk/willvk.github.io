A district of [[The Shallows]], home to the market roads leading up to [[Upper Hammerfall]]. Best known as the location of the Hollow Coin, the tavern and fighting pit built over the sealed entrance to [[Freya]]'s temple, Galanthas — the Flower Gate.

Also home to the Notched Axe, the tavern where Ulf was left behind after the arch into Freya's temple accepted his companions and locked him out.
